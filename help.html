Orders & Delivery

How do I track my order? ▾

You can track your order by visiting the Order Tracking page and entering your order ID. You'll receive real-time updates on your order status including location of the delivery vehicle.

What is the delivery time? ▾

Express delivery: Next day for Dhaka orders placed before 6PM. Standard delivery: 2-5 working days nationwide. Free delivery on orders above ৳1000.

Can I change my delivery address? ▾

Yes, you can change your delivery address before the order is shipped. Go to My Orders, select the order and click "Change Address". Once shipped, address cannot be changed.

What happens if I miss my delivery? ▾

Our delivery agent will attempt delivery 3 times. After 3 failed attempts, your order will be returned to the warehouse. You can reschedule or request a refund.

Returns & Payments

What is the return policy? ▾

Most products have a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. Electronics can be returned within 7 days if sealed. Fashion items can be returned if tags are intact. Some categories may have different policies.

How do I get a refund? ▾

After initiating a return, your refund will be processed within 5-7 working days. Refunds go back to the original payment method — bKash, card, or Nagad. COD orders receive bKash refunds.

What payment methods are accepted? ▾

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard), and Net Banking. All major Bangladesh bank cards are supported.

Is Cash on Delivery available? ▾

Yes! Cash on Delivery is available for all orders across Bangladesh. No additional charge for COD. Simply pay when your order arrives at your door.
