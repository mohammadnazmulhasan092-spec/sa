Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra vs iPhone 15 Pro Max: Which Should You Buy in Bangladesh (2025)?

A
Arif Rahman · Tech Editor
June 14, 2025 8 min read 8,234 views
📱 ⚔️ 📱
TL;DR: For camera quality in Bangladesh conditions, Samsung wins. For ecosystem integration and privacy, iPhone wins. For pure value, Samsung Galaxy A55 5G at ৳28,999 beats them both!

The two most anticipated flagship smartphones of 2025 have finally arrived in Bangladesh. We spent three weeks putting them through grueling real-world tests in Dhaka's weather conditions, and we're ready to give you our definitive verdict.

📱 Design & Build Quality

Both phones are stunning pieces of engineering, but Samsung's Galaxy S24 Ultra takes a bolder approach with its titanium frame and integrated S Pen stylus. The iPhone 15 Pro Max, on the other hand, opts for a more understated titanium design with a USB-C port finally replacing Lightning.

In Bangladesh's monsoon season, both phones carry IP68 water resistance, so you don't need to worry about sudden rains. The S24 Ultra is notably larger at 6.8 inches, while the iPhone at 6.7 inches is marginally easier to hold.

📷 Camera Performance

This is where things get interesting. Samsung's 200MP main camera with 5x optical zoom produces jaw-dropping detail in daylight, while the iPhone's more restrained approach to computational photography gives more natural-looking images that are easier to share without editing.

Under Bangladesh's challenging lighting conditions — harsh midday sun and evening markets — Samsung slightly edges out for zoom capability, while iPhone wins for consistency.
