About Samsung Galaxy A55 5G

The Samsung Galaxy A55 5G is a powerful mid-range smartphone featuring a stunning Super AMOLED display, advanced AI cameras, and a long-lasting battery. Designed to deliver a premium experience at an accessible price point.

  • 6.6-inch Super AMOLED FHD+ display with 120Hz refresh rate for ultra-smooth scrolling
  • 50MP main camera with OIS + 12MP ultra-wide + 5MP macro for versatile photography
  • Exynos 1480 processor for fast and efficient performance
  • 5000mAh battery with 25W fast charging support
  • IP67 water and dust resistance for worry-free everyday use
  • Android 14 with One UI 6.1 for the latest features and updates
  • Corning Gorilla Glass Victus+ for superior screen protection
