OUR STORY

From a Small Idea to Bangladesh's Biggest Marketplace

Founded in 2018 by a group of entrepreneurs passionate about changing how Bangladesh shops, Dadar Shop started as a small online platform selling electronics in Dhaka.

Today, we serve over 10 million customers across all 64 districts of Bangladesh, with hundreds of thousands of products across every category imaginable — from everyday groceries to luxury electronics.

Our team of 2,000+ passionate employees works every day to make shopping easier, faster, and more affordable for every Bangladeshi family.

🛍️
🎯

Our Mission

To empower every Bangladeshi by giving them access to quality products at fair prices, backed by exceptional service and fast delivery — making eCommerce as simple as shopping at your neighborhood store.

🔮

Our Vision

To become the most loved and trusted eCommerce platform in South Asia — a marketplace where small businesses thrive, consumers get the best deals, and technology bridges the gap between buyers and sellers.
